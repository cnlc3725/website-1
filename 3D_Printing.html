December 2020-June 2022

I started with a Labists X1 Mini, with a maximum print size of 100x100x100mm. I used the labists software for it, and the case on the printer head broke. The printer was also very slow, only supported PLA filament, and could not hold any filament roll of the normal size, so you would have to buy the smaller rolls for them to fit. This was a good printer for $100, and was easy to use as a beginner, but was lacking a lot of things that most 3D printers would beefit from. We could have bought osme upgrades for this, but we decided it was time for a new printer.
